About Us

Your Backyard Escape — Since 1992

Since 1992, Paradise Spas has been a family-owned business dedicated to helping customers create the perfect backyard escape. As the oldest spa store in North Dakota, we're known for our unmatched customer service, large selection of hot tubs and swim spas, and long-term support.

We service every spa we sell — no matter its age. We proudly serve customers across the Midwest and beyond, delivering a personal experience built on trust, care, and lasting relationships.
