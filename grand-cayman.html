About This Spa

The Artesian Grand Cayman is one of our most popular models at Paradise Spas — and it's easy to see why. The open barrier seating layout means everyone has a great seat, and the footblaster dome adds a level of foot massage you don't find in most spas at this price point. With the Elite trim, you're getting four jet pumps, DirectFlow Personal Control so each person can adjust their own jets independently, and extended warranty coverage.


We've been selling and servicing Artesian Spas since 1992 and we carry this exact model in our showroom. You're welcome to come in and do a wet test before you commit — that's the only way to really know if a spa is right for you. We handle delivery and installation across North Dakota and the surrounding Midwest. Financing is available with quick approvals. Call us or fill out the form to get current pricing — we run promotions throughout the year and we want to make sure you get the best deal available.
